# Amazon Second Chance - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 2.0

---

# 1. Executive Summary

Amazon Second Chance is Amazon's AI-powered circular commerce platform that gives every product a meaningful second life.

The platform intelligently routes returned, unused, exchanged, and customer-owned products into the most valuable and sustainable outcome:

* Resell
* Refurbish
* Donate
* Recycle
* Exchange

Amazon Second Chance combines AI-powered product grading, sustainable commerce, local Kirana partnerships, NGO collaboration, and peer-to-peer resale into a single ecosystem.

The goal is to transform reverse logistics from a cost center into a value-generating circular economy platform.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Millions of products purchased online are:

* Returned
* Underutilized
* Discarded

despite remaining functional.

This causes:

* Revenue loss
* High return processing costs
* Environmental waste
* Customer distrust of refurbished products
* Inefficient reverse logistics

Current Flow:

Purchase
↓
Return
↓
Warehouse
↓
Disposal

Desired Flow:

Purchase
↓
Use
↓
Return / Idle Product
↓
AI Evaluation
↓
Resell / Refurbish / Donate / Recycle / Exchange
↓
Next Owner
